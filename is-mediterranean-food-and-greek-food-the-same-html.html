What is the origin of the name Mediterranean?

Name. The name Mediterranean comes from the Latin mediterraneus, which means ‘inland’ (medius, which means’middle’ + terra, which means ‘land, earth’), which is derived from the Greek “mesogeios,” which means “middle earth.” The Romans, for example, referred to it as Mare Nostrum (Latin for “Our Sea”) and Mare Internum (Latin for “Internal Sea”) on occasion.
