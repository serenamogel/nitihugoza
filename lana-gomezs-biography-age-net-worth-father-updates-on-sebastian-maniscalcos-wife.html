Lana Gomez’s biography, age, net worth, father, updates on Sebastian Maniscalco’s wife

Lana Gomez is an American painter who has gained prominence in her career. She is one of the most famous painters in the US. She made it to the limelight following her marriage to poplar comedian Sebastian Maniscalco.

Lana has been in love with art since when she was young, and this made her bagged a degree from the University in regards to art.

Continue reading this article to know more about Lana Gomez.

Profile Summary

Lana Gomez’s Biography

The popular painter was born on the 23rd of April 1983 in Naples, Florida, the United States, to Simone Lutgert Gomez and Barry Gomez. She clocked the age of 38 in 2021 and currently resides in Los Angeles, California, USA.

Lana Gomez has two siblings, a brother named Rodney Weiss and a sister named Heather Dockweiler.

The reports revealed that her parents separated when she was nine, and her mother went on to marry Scott Lutgert, who is now her stepfather. Her real dad, Barry Gomez, is deceased.

Education

She attended Rollins College between 2001 and 2002 before she moved to Florida Gulf Coast University, where she was between 2003 to 2004. She later moved to the University of Tennessee, where she started using watercolour; she graduated from the school in 2006 with a bachelor of Arts degree in Studio Art.

Career

After her graduation, Lana Gomez moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career in painting which she did for years before coming in contact with Kelly Wearstler, a famous interior designer. After they met, the two collaborated on different designs, and Lana worked for Kelly’s design studio for a year between 2008 and 2009. Afterwards, she was employed to make a mural-sized piece and also featured in the book entitled “Hue”, which further increased her net worth.

With time, she started gaining attention and has been featured in magazines like InStyle, House Beautiful and The New Yorker, among others.

She, however, made it to the limelight after she married Sebastian in 2013. She became the creative director for his comedy company and now runs her online art magazine.

How she met Sebastian Maniscalco

According to Lana, she revealed that she and Sebastian met at a gym where they trained under the same trainer. She never paid attention to Sebastian, but the comedian was able to use his craft to get her attention, and that was how they started dating. They finally got married on 26 August 2013 and are blessed with two kids named; Serafina Simone Maiscalco and Caruso Jack Maniscalco.
