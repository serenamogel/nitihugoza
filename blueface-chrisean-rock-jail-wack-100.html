Wack 100 Blames Chrisean Rock For Blueface Going To Jail, Says He'll Be Home By Spring

Wack 100 has pointed the finger at Chrisean Rock for being the reason Blueface is behind bars once again.

Wack called out Blueface’s ex for showing up to jail to “support” him on Monday (January 15) when, he claimed, she’s behind “Thotiana” rapper violating his probation in the first place.

“CAP JUST A CLOUT MOVE. SHES THE REASON HE IN THERE. KNOCKOFF THE CAP,” Wack commented on a clip featuring footage of Chrisean with her son visiting Blue in court.

He also pushed back against the rumored July 2 release date for Blueface, claiming the rapper will be home for spring break.

“Cap!! He won’t see the summer. We’ll be partying before spring break.”

Blueface’s mother, Carlissa, claimed that Chrisean Rock brought her son — who Blueface has denied paternity of — to court with here in hopes of Blue securing a lighter sentence.

“We want house arrest. That’s all we want, for him to be at home with these kids. She took that baby in there so that judge would have leniency on his time.

“You gotta follow what the bitch did today. The judge didn’t wanna see his mommy. The judge wanted to see him standing up with somebody holding a brand new baby.”

Footage emerged of a jail phone call with Blueface, who said he was enjoying his time behind bars and expects to be home sooner than people think.

“I love it here. It’s active. It’s cracking,” he claimed. “You know I’ma be home soon. Way before the date.”

On Monday (January 15), TMZ reported that the Los Angeles native is being held in the county jail for violating his probation in an assault charge from 2021 wherein he and his entourage allegedly assaulted a club bouncer.

Though it is unclear what exactly landed him in jail, Blueface’s mother hinted that it may have something to do with “baby mama drama.”

On the same day he turned himself in, Karlissa Saffold took to social media and suggested that Jaidyn Alexis and Chrisean Rock had a hand in her son’s current situation.

“Jaidyn got him one charge, Rock got him the one that violated the other,” she wrote. “That’s why it’s so quiet. Praying everyone now understands why I didn’t want them together. Because parents want the best for their children.”

The 51-year-old continued: “I love them all but not together. Praying for everyone to listen to their parents [prayer hands emoji]. My baby is in jail and my heart is broken again. Because they don’t listen.”
