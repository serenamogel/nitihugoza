Matthew Hagee Biography

Pastor Matthew Hagee (b. July 22, 1978) is an American pastor, author, and singer who was born and brought up in San Antonio, TX, United States of America. Currently, Hagee works at Global Evangelism Incorporated owned Cornerstone Church in San Antonio, TX, as the executive pastor. Hagee has authored “Shaken, Not Shattered” and “Response-Able“.

Pastor Mathew partners with his father Pastor John Hagee (the founder and senior pastor of John Hagee Ministries Church) to telecast their teachings worldwide at GETV.org. He is committed to preaching the Gospel to the whole world and generation. Due to his love for music, he founded the Dove-Award winning gospel quartet, Canton Junction, and Difference Media which is a music label.  He also regularly records and tours with his family’s vocal group, “The Hagee’s” and has released several songs like; He Still Speaks and I Count It, Joy.
