Trina, whose real name is Katrina Laverne Taylor, was born on December 3rd, 1974, in Miami, Florida. At 46 years old, she has made a significant mark in the music industry, especially in the rap genre. Her journey began when she featured on a song with Trick Daddy, which gained success on the charts. Following this, she released her own albums, earning the title of “the most consistent female rapper of all time” by XXL in 20Additionally, Trina has been a part of VH1’s Love and Hip Hop: Miami since its first season in 2018.

Trina’s Engagement in 2021 Trina, known for her past relationships with celebrities like Lil Wayne, Kenyon Martin, and French Montana, has found true love in 202She publicly announced her engagement on September 10th, marking a new chapter in her life. Her fiancé, Raymond Taylor, also known as Ray Almighty, has been by her side for the past five years.

Meet Raymond Taylor Raymond

Taylor, a Miami rapper and former college basketball player, is the man who captured Trina’s heart. Despite their 13-year age difference, Raymond, who was 33 in 2021, shares a deep bond with Trina. His Instagram account, @raymondtaylortoo, is filled with affectionate captions, where he calls her “the most gorgeous woman in the entire world” and shares numerous photos of their time together.

A Blossoming Friendship Before their engagement, Raymond and Trina were best friends. Trina herself once stated on Love and Hip Hop: Miami that Raymond was her closest confidant, someone she knew like the back of her hand.

Love and Hip Hop Miami’s Influence Much of Trina’s professional and personal life has been showcased on Love and Hip Hop: Miami. Her relationship with Raymond has also played out on the show, initially appearing as just friends before taking the big step of getting engaged.
