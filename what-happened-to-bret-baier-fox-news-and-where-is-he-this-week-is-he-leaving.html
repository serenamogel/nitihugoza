Bret Baier is the lead political anchor for FOX News Channel (FNC) and the host of “Special Report with Bret Baier”, the highest-rated cable news show in its timeslot and consistently one of the top five programs in cable news.

Baier joined the network in 1998 as the Atlanta bureau’s first reporter.

He covered the 2012 campaign season, anchoring the Presidential and Vice Presidential debates and the Republican and Democratic conventions.
