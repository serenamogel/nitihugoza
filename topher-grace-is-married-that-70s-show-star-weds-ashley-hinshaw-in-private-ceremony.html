That '70s Show Star Topher Grace Is Married

And just like that, Eric Forman is a married man!

That ‘70s Show star Topher Grace married his fiancée Ashley Hinshaw Sunday evening in a private ceremony near Santa Barbara, Calif., according to People.

While the actor previously admitted to E! News that his leading lady was in control of the special ceremony, he was excited for the big day to come.

"I don't do any wedding planning," he told E! News' Marc Malkin. "It is not up to me…I'm just going to show up."

The special day comes about a month after the bride-to-be enjoyed a special bridal shower with her closest friends.
