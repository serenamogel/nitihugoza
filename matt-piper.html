Biography

Matt Piper was born in California on December 19, 1994. OpTic Gaming member known to his online fans as OpTic FormaL, this Call of Duty gamer has more than 260,000 YouTube subscribers. His most viewed YouTube videos include “MY BEST CLUTCH SO FAR” and “OpTic FormaL Montage.” He and Seth Abner (a.k.a. OpTic Scumper) both rose to fame as members of the OpTic Gaming team. At the start of his gaming career, he played Halo.
