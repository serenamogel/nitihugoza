Is Darren Sproles Related To Phoenix Sproles? Family Tree Explored

It is popular knowledge that Darren Sproles is related to Phoenix Sproles, and they are cousins. Darren gives Phoenix tips and tricks every now and then.

Both Darren and Phoenix are football players; the former has already made his mark and left the game, while the latter has just stepped into the fold.

Sproles is a senior wide receiver from New Hope, Minnesota, who completed his college at James Madison University. 

He started his football career in high school at Robbinsdale Cooper High School, where he played wide receiver, strong safety, punter, and punt returner.

Sproles was a four-year letterman and three-year starter in high school before graduating in 2018. He was named all-conference twice and Special Teams Player of the Year.

Before going to the JMU, Sproles was a North Dakota State University student. Phoenix declared his intention to play in the 2024 NFL draft on December 27, 2023. 

On the other hand, Darren Lee Sproles is a former running back and return specialist who now works as a personnel consultant for the Philadelphia Eagles of the National Football League (NFL).

He attended Kansas State University, earning first-team All-American accolades and becoming the school’s all-time best rusher.

Is Darren Sproles Related To Phoenix Sproles Or Not? 

Yes, Darren Sproles is related to Phoenix Sproles. In fact, Phoenix is Darren’s cousin. It looks like football runs in the genes. 

Phoenix Sproles is the son of Efonda Sproles and Chaconas Howard. His brother Jaxon is no stranger to the football world; he established a name for himself in its domain.

Jaxon Howard is a highly regarded football prospect ranked as the No. 1 player in Minnesota for the Class of 2023.

Furthermore, Phoenix has three sisters, Sterling, Aeyva, and Channell. It might be fate, but his mother later married a football player. 

His step-father Willie L. Howard is a former National Football League (NFL) defensive end from the United States.

He was picked in the second round of the 2001 NFL Draft by the Minnesota Vikings and played for them for two seasons before a catastrophic injury ended his career.

Willie was also the football coach of Robbinsdale Cooper High School (Phoenix’s high school).

Darren’s father, Larry Sproles, was a running back at MidAmerica Nazarene University. Unfortunately, Annette, his mother, died of cancer in April 2004.

Meet Darren’s Wife, Michel Hunt

Darren has been happily married to Michel Hunt for a long time. The pair tied the knot on June 17, 2010, in a small ceremony with their close ones. 

Darren is not the only athlete in the relationship, as Michel is a track runner herself. Michel grew up in Los Angeles, California.

She began running track for the Los Angeles Jets at the age of seven and graduated from the program at the age of fourteen.

Michel qualified for the Junior Olympics in both her individual and relay events every season she played for the Jets. 

Furthermore, Michel used the track to help him get a Full Ride Scholarship at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas (UNLV).

She received her Bachelor of Arts in Journalism from UNLV in 2005 and her Master of Arts in Leadership Studies from the University of San Diego.
