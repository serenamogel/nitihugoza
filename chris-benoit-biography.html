Chris Benoit Bio (Wiki)

Who is Chris Benoit?

Chris Benoit was a Canadian WWE professional wrestler. He is known for his tenure with ‘Stampede Wrestling’ promotion as Dynamite.

Benoit was a two-time world champion, one-time WCW World Heavyweight Champion, and a one-time World Heavyweight Champion in WWE.

Furthermore, he became a hot topic on the internet after he allegedly killed his wife Nancy Benoit and their 7-year-old son, Daniel, before hanging himself.

Birth Facts, Early Life, Parents

Chris Benoit was 37 years old, was born on 21 May 1961 as Christopher Michael Benoit in Montreal, Canada. Currently, he would’ve been 60 years old if he was alive.

His father’s name was Michael Benoit and his mother’s name was Margaret Benoit. He grew up in Edmonton, Alberta. Likewise, he also had a sister named Laurie Benoit. He was interested in wrestling since his childhood and decided to pursue a career in Wrestling at the age of 12.

Chris Benoit belonged to Canadian nationality and was born under the birth sign Gemini.

Career, Professional Life

Chris started his professional wrestling career at the age of 24 in 1985 and won Stampede British Commonwealth Mid-Heavyweight Championship. This was followed by the second British Commonwealth title.

After that, he was contracted with Japan Pro-Wrestling (NJPW) in March 1989 where he was famous as The Pegasus Kid. Here, he won several championships matches including Best of Super Juniors and Super J-Cup Tournament.

Similarly, Chris Benoit got fame when he got the opportunity to fight for the ‘World Heavyweight’ title at ‘Wrestlemania 20’ after he won the ‘Royal Rumble’ in 2004. he got his new ring name Finn Balor.

Here he tussled with many WWE stars including Booker T, CM Punk, Bobby Lashley, Rey Mysterio, and many others. Furthermore, he has won the World Heavyweight Championship, WWE United States champion, and ECW World Heavyweight Championship.
