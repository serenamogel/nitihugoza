Chris Matthews Scandal: What Happened To Him?

Chris Matthews is a renowned political commentator, news anchor, author, and retired TV host. He left his TV show Hardball with Chris Matthews after accusations of inappropriate behavior.

Chris is best known for the talk show that came on America’s Talking and later on MSNBC. In March 2020, he announced that he was retiring from the long-running show, saying that the younger generation should take over.

Chris Matthews Scandal: He Retired After Accusation Of Harassment

Chris Matthews had plans to stay on the show Hardball until the completion of the 2020 election. But his dream was cut short, and he announced his retirement in March of that year.

His decision came after a female Journalist accused him of sexual misconduct and inappropriate remarks during her guest appearances on the program in 2016.

Huffington Post reporter Laura Bassett wrote a GQ exposé on the host, calling him out for making “objectifying and belittling comments” toward her and other female guests.

Also, the show was infamous for many controversial encounters and remarks on and off the air.

Chris had received criticisms for several other controversial actions, including his comparison of Bernie Sanders’ victory in the Nevada caucus to the fall of France to the Nazis.

Similarly, in 2007, Chris drew controversy when he claimed that the Bush administration tried to silence him by pressuring MSNBC to stop him from criticizing the Iraq War.

There were also heated confrontations between Chris and guests, such as conservative commentator Michelle Malkin, Senator Zell Miller, and broadcaster Kevin James. 

Following the cancellation of Hardball, the time slot was filled by rotating MSNBC hosts and then by The ReidOut, hosted by Joy Reid.

What Is He Doing Now? Chris Matthews Is Retired

Christ Matthews is mostly laying low and living his retired life. The 76-year-old is living with his wife of 42 years, Kathleen Matthews. The couple has three grown children, Michael, Thomas, and Caroline.

Recently, Chris was a surprise guest commentator on Morning Joe. Although some viewers appreciated his analysis of Donald Trump, many more took to social media to criticize the move from MSNBC.

His return to MSNBC received mixed reactions from everyone.

Many audiences were disappointed with the network for giving a platform to someone who has been a committer of sexual harassment and other controversial comments and actions.

Meanwhile, some audiences were happy that the 76-year-old had returned to TV, mentioning that everyone deserves a second chance.

Net Worth: Chris Matthews Is Still Wealthy

As per Celebrity Net Worth, Chris Matthews has a net worth of $20 million. It has been possible by the vast array of his works.

Thanks to his political involvement as a democratic member, work of around 15 years as a print Journalist, writing of eight best-selling books, and running of his talk show from 1994 to 2020, he is where he is now.

His annual salary from Hardball with Chris Matthews was $5 million. 

From 2002 to 2013, Matthews also hosted the syndicated program The Chris Matthews Show. It was a half-hour weekend political news roundtable show.

Chris was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, on December 17, 1945. From 1994 until 2020, he was professionally active for 26 years.

He is a well-educated man who has completed his graduate course in Economics and received as many as 34 honorary degrees.

For his decades of work, he has received awards and accolades.

It includes The Pennsylvania Society’s Gold Medal, the Abraham Lincoln Award, the David Brinkley Award for Excellence in Broadcast Journalism, the JFK Memorial Award, and the Tip O’Neill Irish Diaspora Award.
