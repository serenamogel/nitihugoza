    How To Make Mozzarella Sticks In Air Fryer

  • Prep breading bowls. In a shallow bowl whisk together the flour, salt, and pepper. In a separate shallow dish, whisk the eggs well. Prepare a third shallow bowl or pie plate with the Panko breadcrumbs, garlic powder, oregano, and paprika, mixing until combined.
  • Bread mozzarella sticks. Cut each mozzarella stick in half. Working one at a time, place a mozzarella stick into the flour and turn to coat well. Dust off any excess flour, then dip into the egg mixture, coating the cheese stick completely. Allow excess egg mixture to drip off. Place the cheese stick in the Panko breading and press the crumbs into the cheese.
  • Freeze. Line the coated cheese sticks onto a parchment lined tray and place in the freezer for at least 30 minutes. Refrigerate the egg mixture between uses.
  • Repeat breading process. Remove from freezer and dip each stick back into the egg mixture, then again in the Panko crumbs. Return to the freezer for an additional 30-60 minutes.
  • Preheat air fryer. Preheat your air fryer to 390¬∞F (199¬∞C) for 5 minutes. Spray the air fryer basket with olive oil spray.
  • Air fry sticks. Place the mozzarella sticks into the bottom of the basket in a single layer, with space between each stick. You will need to work in batches. Spray the mozzarella sticks lightly with olive oil spray. Cook in the air fryer for 5-8 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbling and the breading is crispy.

    Mozzarella Sticks Air Fryer Method FAQs

    Why do I have to double-bread the sticks?

    For the best crunch, and to get as close as possible to fried mozzarella sticks, it is important to bread the cheese sticks twice.

    How do I keep the breading from falling off?

    When breading the cheese sticks, press the breading firmly into the sticks. Freezing the cheese sticks after breading also helps the breading to stay on.

    Can I skip the freezing steps?

    Freezing your mozzarella sticks allows the cheese to stay solid until the breading becomes crispy enough to hold the cheese inside. If you skip the freezing steps your cheese will ooze out.

    I froze the mozzarella sticks but the cheese still melted out. What happened?

    It is important to make sure that the sticks are coated completed with both the egg and the breading mixture. The coating acts as a seal. If there are spots left uncoated then the cheese will seep out. A little cheese may still escape, but coating the cheese stick well will keep most of the cheese inside.

    How do I reheat the mozzarella sticks in air fryer?

    To reheat your mozzarella cheese sticks, place them in the air fryer at 390¬∞F for 3 minutes. 

    Can I freeze the mozzarella sticks to cook at a later time?

    The mozzarella sticks can be frozen uncooked for up to 3 months. Once they are frozen in a single layer, place them in a freezer safe container. There is no need to thaw them before cooking. Place frozen mozzarella sticks in air fryer and follow the instructions below in the recipe card, beginning with step 10.

    Bought frozen mozzarella sticks at the store? Those need to air fry for the same time and at the same temperature. So about 5 to 8 minutes at 390¬∞F.

    How To Store

    In the freezer. Uncooked mozzarella sticks can be frozen for up to 3 months. Freeze in a single layer for 30 minutes and then transfer to a freezer safe container such as a ziploc bag.

    In the fridge. Leftovers can be stored in the fridge for 3 days and then reheated in the air fryer.
