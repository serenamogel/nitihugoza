Andre-Frank professional path

In 2015, Zambo joined Ligue 1 team Marseille. On September 17, 2015, he made his UEFA Europa League debut against FC Groningen. He debuted in Ligue 1 against Lyon. In 2018, he joined the English Premier League team Fulham. Following the club’s relegation, he was loaned to Villarreal FC, where he made his debut on August 17, 2019, against Granada CF. He returned to Fulham by signing a three-year deal. He was loaned out to Napoli once again. He was permanently moved to Napoli in May 2022 after signing a four-year deal. He also represented Cameroon in the 2017 Confederations Cup and the 2021 AFCON tournament.
