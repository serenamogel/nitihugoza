Jill Scott Husband: Who Is Jill Scotts Husband? Is Jill Scott currently married?

Jill Heather Scott is an American singer, songwriter, model, poet and actress.

Jill Scott’s 2000 debut album, Who Is Jill Scott?: Words and Sounds Vol. 1, went platinum, and the follow-ups Beautifully Human: Words and Sounds Vol. 2 and The Real Thing: Words and Sounds Vol. 3 both achieved gold status.

Jill Scott made her film debut in 2007 in Hounddog and Tyler Perry’s Why Did I Get Married?

In 2008, Jill Scott starred in the BBC/HBO series The No. 1 Ladies’ Detective Agency, based on the novels of the same name by Alexander McCall Smith.

After a four-year hiatus from music, Jill Scott released her fourth album, The Light of the Sun, in 2011.

In 2014, Jill Scott starred in the film Get on Up as the second wife of James Brown and in 2015, she released her fifth album, Woman.

In 2018, Jill Scott appeared in The CW DC Comics superhero series Black Lightning as Lady Eve.
