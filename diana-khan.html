Diana Khan is an Indian actress who works in the Hindi TV industry and has acted in various TV serials. She has also worked in movies for which she got immense love and huge recognition. She was born on 27th December 1999, hailing from Maharashtra, India where she raised and grew up. Later, she moved to Mumbai where she took the start of her career with a small screen. Initially, Diana Khan made an appearance on the TV and started appearing in many serials.

Career

One of her interviews, Diana Khan revealed she took inspiration from Akshay Kumar and Salman Khan. While she also admires stunning Priyanka Chopra Jonas. Being a passionate actress, she always looks for golden opportunities to work on. She is mainly known for playing a small role in “Humsafar” for which she was praised by many fans and viewers.

Meanwhile, she also became the cast of the TV show “Parvarish” in which she portrayed the role of Jaswinder Khurana in series and her fans were noted for her amazing performance. She plays each role brilliantly for which she always wins the hearts of many with her incredible acting. Apart from this, she has performed in many commercials and advertisements for Cadbury. Also, she worked in “Gumraah” which was aired on V Channel. Moreover, she is known for her amazing contribution to the Indian cinema and TV industry.

She is known for her versatility and wonderful acting prowess for which she received a lot of fame. She has now reached the peak of success.

Achievements

Diana Khan appeared in the movie named “Ghayal Once Again” in which she worked alongside Sunny Deol, which is a very big achievement for her.
