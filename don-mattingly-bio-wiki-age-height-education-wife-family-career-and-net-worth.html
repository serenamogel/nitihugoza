Don Mattingly Biography

Donald Mattingly is an American former professional baseball player and coach who was born and brought up in Evansville, Indiana, U.S. He is the bench coach for the Toronto Blue Jays. He spent his 14-year MLB career playing with the New York Yankees.

    Mattingly played professional baseball for 14 seasons, all with the New York Yankees. He was a nine-time All-Star and won the American League Most Valuable Player award in 1985. After retiring from playing, Mattingly became a coach and manager in Major League Baseball. He has managed the Los Angeles Dodgers and the Miami Marlins.
