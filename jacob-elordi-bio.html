Jacob Elordi was born on June 26, 1997 who is an Australian actor famous for playing role as Noah Flynn in The Kissing Booth franchise (2018–2021). He is also famous for the role Nate Jacobs in the ongoing HBO series Euphoria (2019–present).

Early Life

Elordi was born in Brisbane, Queensland, Australia. His father immigrated from the Basque Country at the age of 8. He grew up with 3 older sisters and received his education from St. Kevin’s College in Melbourne and St. Joseph’s College in Nudgee, Brisbane.

Career

Jacob Elordi first gained huge exposure from the movie i.e., Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Men Tell No Tales (role, as an extra). But later, he was official casted for role Rooster in Swinging Safari (2018) Australian film.

Elordi’s was gaining very popularity after he played the role of Noah Flynn in the romantic comedy film franchise The Kissing Booth.

In 2019, Elordi started on his journey as Nate Jacobs in the HBO drama series Euphoria. He has also made an appearance in the horror film i.e., The Mortuary Collection.

In 2022, Elordi starred in Adrian Lyne’s sensual thriller Deep Water. He continued to rise by taking on the lead role in Saltburn (2023) as Elvis Presley.
